# Getting access to the shared team email

(as of 2025)

> **Important:** You have to contact the CeBiTec support (or me) to have your account added to the permitted users. 

## Webmail (browser)

Log in at https://webmail.cebitec.uni-bielefeld.de/ with your own email address. You do not need to enter the
"verification code". And you're done!

## Thunderbird (all operating systems)

-> Log in with your own CeBiTec email address and password.

Then, under the folders (Inbox, Spam, etc.) in the left-hand sidebar, there should be a folder called
"Beschreibungen". When you open it, there is a subfolder called "Other Users", which
in turn has a subfolder called "igem-info". This is the iGem inbox.

### Problem: Subfolders are not visible

That is because they are not visible. 

-  Go to "Settings" und "Server Settings"
- Gehe zu den erweiterten Einstellungen 
- Uncheck the box next to "Show subscribed folders only".


The iGem folder should now be visible.

## Geary (Linux)

- Click on Add account. 
- Enter your CeBiTec address as your email address. 
- Enter your CeBiTec name as your login name. 
- Enter your CeBiTec password as your password.

Then, in the sidebar on the left, under the folders (Inbox, Spam, etc.), there should be a folder called
"Descriptions". When you open it, there is a subfolder called "Other Users", which
in turn has a subfolder called "igem-info". This is the iGem inbox.
